FLR Finance

FLR Finance is an ecosystem of smart contract protocols, deployed on the Flare Network. We help ensure that FLR Finance can uphold its promise to keep its community safe by delivering concrete recommendations for improvements of its protocols, codebase, and operational security, by ensuring high code quality, and by enhancing its testing pipeline. Our smart contract audits and technical reports are published openly for dissemination within the community. Additionally, we develop novel protocols that expand the FLR Finance ecosystem even further.
