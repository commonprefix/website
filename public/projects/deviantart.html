deviantART

deviantART has been the most popular social network for artists since the early 2000s. As a caretaker of an enormous library of high-quality digital art, deviantART has a thriving community of artists. In the Blockchain Era, artists are facing multiple challenges such as cases where their art is tokenized and uploaded as an NFT without their authorization. We helped deviantART scope and develop their “NFT Protect” project, which scans public blockchains for NFT mints that match their existing database of artworks. Furthermore, we are actively working closely with deviantART to flesh out their business and product strategy in a world where art is becoming increasingly tokenized by the day.
