gumi Cryptos Capital

gumi Cryptos Capital (gCC) is a blockchain-focused venture capital firm with headquarters in Silicon Valley and Tokyo. Common Prefix partners with gumi to perform scouting and due diligence on new candidate investments in all blockchain areas, from zero knowledge to interoperability, and across diverse ecosystems from Ethereum to Cosmos. We apply our scientific approach to help them make informed investment decisions by providing in-depth technical and scientific diligence. Additionally, we support gumi's fast-expanding portfolio which ranges from tiny moonshot start-ups to well-established DeFi and gaming companies in the space.
