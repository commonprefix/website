Pantos

Pantos is a blockchain interoperability project designed to facilitate the seamless transfer of tokens and assets across different blockchain networks without relying on a trusted third-party intermediary. Launched by the team behind Bitpanda (the most prominent European cryptocurrency exchange), Pantos is developing a protocol powered by the Pantos Token (PAN), a native multichain token that eases interoperability between various blockchains such as Ethereum, BNB Chain, Avalanche, Polygon, and more.

Pantos smart contract audit

Common Prefix was commissioned to conduct a security audit of the Pantos Solidity smart contracts. This protocol enables seamless bridging of tokens adhering to the Pantos Digital Asset Standard (PANDAS) across EVM-compatible chains. The audit also included a review of wrapper contracts, which allow any ERC-20 or EVM-native token to be wrapped to comply with the Pantos standard.
